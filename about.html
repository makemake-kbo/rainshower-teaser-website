About us

Rainshower Labs is dedicated to creating innovative DeFi protocols and primitives that promote trustless-ness. Our flagship product, Rainshower, is an undercollateralized lending protocol that allows users to access loans with high flexibility and capital efficency. We believe in the core principles of crypto, and we're dedicated to building secure and reliable protocols that empower users.
